ABOUT ME

Hi there!


I'm a full-stack web developer based in New York City with experience building apps in React / Redux and Ruby on Rails.


My favorite part of programming is the never-ending sense that there's always something new to master --- a secret language feature, an established design pattern, an emerging library or -- most importantly -- a different way of looking at a problem. It's like being an artist who gains a new set of paintbrushes with every hour of effort. I haven't experienced a hobby that more actively encourages the constant thrill of discovery.


In my spare time, I can be found producing courses on Udemy, playing guitar, and using spreadsheets to crack Taylor Swift's songwriting formulas (really.)
